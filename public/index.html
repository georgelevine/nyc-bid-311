Where the data comes from

Each pin comes from NYC Open Data (official record, ~1-day lag), the live 311 Portal (near-realtime), or both. Portal queries are split across daily windows and sub-divided spatially when needed so the portal's per-request limit doesn't truncate results. Requests seen in both sources are counted once.
